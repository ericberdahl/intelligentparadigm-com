Analog Input/Output Module

A Video Explorer 2 base card with Analog I/O Daughterboard allows you to work directly with analog video equipment such as analog video tape recorders, monitors and other peripherals.

Analog I/O Daughterboard Features include:

    Input

    • dual analog video inputs
    • Internal color space conversion
    • Programmable peak white, hue, brightness, saturation, and contrast for each input.

    Output

    • Simultaneous Composite, Y/C, and Component Outputs (YUV or RGB)
    • Programmable chroma and luna filters
    • Complete NTSC and PAL support
