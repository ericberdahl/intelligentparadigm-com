Primatte Explorer takes the most difficult elements in a foreground shot—smoke, fog, water droplets, hair wisps, transparent and translucent materials—and easily composites them into the background footage. See for yourself the true matting quality that other keying tools can’t touch.

Primatte Explorer software is based on IMAGICA’s chroma matting application that has been used for special effects in numerous movies, television shows and commercials. The Primatte Explorer real-time engine is powered by Intelligent Paradigm’s patented MSIC™ video processing technology, which provides power and precision far beyond current broadcast-quality video requirements.
