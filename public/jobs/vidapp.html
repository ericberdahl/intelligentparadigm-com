Video Application Engineer

Major Responsibilities:

You will be a member of a small team developing cross-platform applications for delivering broadcast quality digital video effects and video processing applications to the commercial marketplace. You will participate in system-level and software design reviews.

Skills/Knowledge Requirements:

  • You should have a minimum of 2 years experience developing applications on at least one of our main delivery platforms (MacOS, Windows NT, Linux)
  • Cross-platform experience helpful
  • Experience with at least one major application framework (e.g. PowerPlant, MFC, GTK+) desired
  • Knowledge of video broadcasting standards (PAL, NTSC, HD) a bonus
  • Experience with audio and video editing environments a big plus
  • Experience with QuickTime helpful

Intelligent Paradigm, Inc. is a digital media company with offices in Cupertino, California, and Schaumburg, Illinois. Founded in 1996, the company aims to establish a new paradigm for digital media with the introduction of MSIC video processing chips and the Video Explorer 2 broadcast video processing board.
